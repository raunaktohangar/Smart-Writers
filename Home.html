What is collaborative story writing?

Collaborative story writing, also known as collective or group storytelling, is a creative process in which multiple individuals contribute to the development of a story. It involves a group of people working together to collectively write a narrative, often taking turns or collaborating in real-time to shape the plot, characters, and setting.
Collaborative story writing can be a fun activity among friends, a creative exercise in writing workshops or classrooms, or even a way for authors to co-write a novel or a series. It encourages teamwork, communication, and the ability to adapt to different writing styles and ideas, making it a dynamic and collaborative process.
